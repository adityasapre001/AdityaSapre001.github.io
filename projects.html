  1. Understanding the fundamental interactions between fungal spores, Lignin Nanoparticles and their combined effect over rose petal infectivity. (August 2018 to present)
    Principal Investigator: Dr Orlin D Velev (NC State University) We are trying to study the fundamental properties of environmentally benign Lignin Nanoparticles, their size distributions using dynamic light scattering and surface charges using Zeta potential measurements. We are trying to analyse the Brotrytis fungal growth and interactions with lignin nanoparticles over potato dextrose agar plates with the help of various types of microscopy techniques (optical and confocal microscopy), SEM. We have chosen rose petals as the model biological surface to macroscopically observe the overall infectivity and the antifungal properties of EbNPs.
  2. Observing the aggregation trends of Chymotrypsinogen and Immunoglobulin G using dynamic light scattering (May 2019 to present)
    Principal Investigator: Dr Orlin D Velev (NC State University) Industrial collaborator: Dr James McAndrew Prevention of the denaturation or aggregation of a protein chain is a conundrum in front of the world research community. Thus, here we are trying to study the fundamentals behind protein aggregation based on their hydrophobic interactions and chain folding behaviors. Also the work involves examination of the effects of various solvents on protein aggregation using dynamic light scattering.
  3. Black Box Optimisation Competition - NeurIPS 2020
    In this project we studied Bayesian Optimisation and its application in deep learning. We studied trust regions based optimisation and designed an algorithm for hyperparameter optimisation which can work with any deep learning algorithm.
  4. Document Visual Question Answering Challenge(DocVQA) - CVPR 2020
    In this project we used a variation of BERT which jointly models text and layout information to effectively answer questions based on scanned Document Images. Our method ranks 4th on the leaderboard on the novel dataset provided by competition authors against very competitive academic and commercial research labs.
  5. Face Deduplication
    In this project, I worked on detecting if a new face entry is already part of a database of 40 million faces. To do it in real time for commercial use, our models ran in less than 0.3 seconds - HyperVerge
  6. Text Extraction OCR
    Worked on buiding new OCR models for national identity cards for 2 countries. The aim was to automate the information extraction task for national identity cards - HyperVerge
